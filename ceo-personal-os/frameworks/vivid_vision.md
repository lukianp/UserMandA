# Vivid Vision (Inspired by Tony Robbins)

Tony Robbins teaches creating a "Vivid Vision" – a detailed, emotionally vivid description of your ideal future, written as if it's happening now. It's not about setting goals; it's about experiencing success in advance to make it magnetic.

For busy CEOs, this framework cuts through the noise, helping you stay focused on what truly matters beyond the quarterly numbers.

## Why Use Vivid Vision

- **Motivation**: Daily visualization builds unstoppable drive.
- **Clarity**: Forces you to define success in personal terms.
- **Emotional Anchor**: Ties your work to deeper fulfillment.

## How to Create Your Vivid Vision

Take 30-60 minutes in a quiet space. Write in the first person, present tense ("I am," "I feel," "I see"). Engage all senses: sights, sounds, smells, textures, emotions. Be extravagant – no holding back.

Read it aloud weekly. Update annually as your vision evolves.

## Template Prompts

Start with your ideal day, then expand to year and life.

### Your Ideal Day as CEO

It's a typical day in your achieved vision. Describe it vividly:

- What time do you wake? How does your body feel? What's the first thing you do?
- Describe your morning routine: Coffee? Meditation? Family time?
- At work: What does your office or workspace look like? Who is on your team? What key decisions are you making?
- Interactions: How do you communicate with your board, employees, partners? What feedback do you receive?
- Evenings: How do you unwind? Family dinners, hobbies, reflection?
- Feelings: What emotions dominate? Pride, peace, excitement?

### Your Ideal Year

Zoom out: What does this year in your vision look like?

- Professional milestones: Launches, impacts, recognitions.
- Personal growth: Relationships deepened, health optimized.
- Finances: Freedom to invest in passions.
- Fun: Adventures, celebrations, downtime.

### Your Ideal Life

The big picture: Legacy and balance.

- Career: How has [YOUR COMPANY] or your industry changed because of you?
- Relationships: Deep connections with family, mentors, community.
- Health: Peak physical and mental state.
- Finances: Wealth used for good.
- Meaning: Contributions that outlast you.
- Fun: Endless sources of joy.

### Sensory Details

Make it real: The sound of applause at a keynote, the smell of fresh ocean air on vacation, the warmth of a partner's hand.

### Emotional Core

End with: "I feel [emotion] because [reason]. This is the life I deserve and create."

## Integration

This Vivid Vision feeds into your North Star. Use it to inspire your 10-year goals. Revisit during annual reviews to refresh.
