# Identity and Values Interview

This interview helps you clarify your core identity and values. As a CEO, it's easy to define yourself by your role or achievements. This digs deeper: Who are you beyond the title? What principles guide you?

Do this once at the start, then annually. Takes 30-45 minutes. Write honestly.

## Guided Questions

1. **What roles define you?** Beyond CEO – father/mother, friend, innovator, etc. Which are most important?

## Synthesis

Summarize your core identity in 3-5 sentences. List your top 3-5 values. Use this to align decisions and goals.
