# Daily Check-In Template

Use this template for your end-of-day reflection. Copy its contents into a new file named with today's date (e.g., `2025-01-01.md`) in the `reviews/daily/` folder. Takes under 5 minutes. Do it consistently to build clarity and momentum.

**Date:** [Today's Date]

**Energy Level (1-10):** [Rate your overall energy. 1 = exhausted, 10 = vibrant]  
*Notes:* [What influenced this? Sleep, meetings, exercise?]

**One Meaningful Win:** [Highlight one thing that moved you forward today. Could be a decision, conversation, or small victory.]

**One Friction Point:** [Identify one obstacle or annoyance. What caused it? How did it feel?]

**One Thing to Let Go Of:** [A worry, task, or thought to release before bed. Why hold onto it?]

**One Priority for Tomorrow:** [The single most important focus for tomorrow. Keep it actionable.]

*Optional Reflection:* [Any other thoughts? Gratitude or adjustments?]

This check-in grounds you in what's working and what needs attention. Review past entries monthly for patterns.
